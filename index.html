About QShare

QShare

QShare is a up and coming app that aims to provide users the ability to create collaborative queues for songs and podcasts. It works by the user connecting their Spotify or (Soon) Apple Music to the app and creating a Queue/Party. These Parties and Queues can be joined by other users who can collarborate on the Queue either by voting or adding the next song or podcast, removing the need for a single aux or DJ while allowing everyone to listen to something enjoyable.
